About Us

Founded on September 19, 2021, BGMI IT began with a powerful mission: to empower individuals—especially the educated but unemployed—with the tools, training, and mindset needed to thrive in the digital economy. We believe that technology should not be limited to the few, but made accessible to everyone. Whether you are a university graduate unsure of your next steps, or someone dreaming of a freelancing career, BGMI IT is here to guide your path. Our institute is more than just a training center. We are building a full-fledged IT firm where learning, creating, and working happen together. Our dual focus allows us to serve both students and clients—creating a unique ecosystem where learners gain experience by solving real-world problems.

What We Offer?

At BGMI IT, we currently offer high-demand courses that lead to real-world jobs and freelancing income:

  • - Web Development
  • - Full Stack Developer
  • - Frontend Developer
  • - Graphic Design
  • - Video Editing
  • - Digital Marketing
  • - Motion Graphics
  • - UX/UI Design

Each course is designed with hands-on practice, guided by instructors who are active professionals and freelancers in their fields. We don’t just teach—you’ll learn by doing.
