Social Media Marketing (SMM) refers to the marketing of products, services, and brands on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and TikTok. The course teaches how to build audience engagement, create content strategy, manage ad campaigns, and utilize analytics for growth. In this course, you'll Learn Principles of Social Media Marketing, Platform-specific, Platform-specific strategies (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.), Paid Advertising via Meta Ads Manager, TikTok Ads, Google Ads, Content Creation using Canva, CapCut, and ChatGPT, Post Scheduling and Automation (Hootsuite, Buffer), Influencer Marketing & Branding, Analytics and Reporting (Google Analytics, Meta Insights), Campaign strategy and performance optimization.
